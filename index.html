Why It Matters

Satellite Operations

High solar wind speeds and geomagnetic storms can affect satellite electronics, GPS signals, and communication systems.

Power Grids

Strong geomagnetic disturbances increase risks to transformers and ground-based power systems, potentially causing blackouts.

Astronaut Safety

Radiation from solar storms poses health risks to astronauts and requires mission adjustments during strong space weather events.
